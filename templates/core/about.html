About Egalitarian Computers

On a mission to make world-class tech education accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

A Learned Society Is a Progressive Society

Egalitarian Computers — operating its edutech platform as Tech E (Tech Egalitarian) — was founded on the belief that access to quality technology education should not be limited by geography, income, or background.

We believe every single person contributes to human advancement, and that a generation of confident, skilled problem-solvers is the foundation of a better world.

Tech Egalitarian (Tech E)

A subsidiary of Egalitarian Computers — operating as a global online learning network serving learners in person and digitally across Africa and beyond.

Our Story

Egalitarian Computers was established in Lagos, Nigeria, by Michael Nnadi and Edwin Osamezu. What started as a local training centre at Oyekola Shopping Complex, Jakande Gate, Oke-Afa Isolo, has grown into a global education network reaching learners in over 150 countries.

The platform offers both in-person and online instruction — spanning foundational IT skills, full-stack web development, data science, blockchain engineering, and more. Every course is built to take a student from zero to job-ready.

As the platform grows, we remain committed to our founding principle: no one should be excluded from the benefits of technology education because of where they were born or what they can afford.
